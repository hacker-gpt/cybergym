Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

Our main training floor is open 24/7 for members. Our staffed front desk and coaching team are on site from 6am to 10pm, seven days a week.

Do I need a membership, or can I drop in?

Both. We offer flexible monthly and annual memberships, and you can also grab a single-day pass to try us out before you commit.

What is included in a membership?

Unlimited access to the weight floor, cardio and conditioning equipment, group classes, locker rooms, and our dedicated recovery zone.

Do you offer personal training?

Yes. Our certified coaches build personalized programs and offer one-on-one and small-group sessions. Ask the front desk to get matched with a coach who fits your goals.

Can I freeze or cancel my membership?

Absolutely. You can pause your membership for up to three months a year, or cancel anytime — there is no long-term contract.

What should I bring for my first visit?

Just comfortable workout clothes, athletic shoes, a water bottle, and a towel. Lockers and showers are available on site, so you are ready to train the moment you walk in.
